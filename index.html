Hiking Mt.Sneffels

Scroll Down

Colorado Springs

After an incredibly long drive, me and my girlfriend made our first stop in Colorado Springs to see the Garden of the Gods. We only stayed briefly since we had 4 more to Telluride where we would begin hiking Mt.Sneffels first thing in the morning.

Beginning of Climb

We had only been hiking for about a mile and views along the way were really amazing. Lush forest and fresh water streams, we made sure to bring Purification tablets so we didn't end up with diarrhea overnight incase anything upstream was contaminating the water.

San Juan Mountains

This was about 3.5 miles up. We had climbed roughly 2,500ft maybe and we were exhausted. My girlfriend didn't have experience hiking so I began carrying her pack along with mine early on in the hike. My legs very weak and I needed some food, but we were so close so we had to continue along to get to the campground where we could finally rest for the night.

Almost There...

Finally we broke the treeline. The entire time up this hike we kept asking hikers on the descent how long we had to go and if the path ever got any easier. They all gave us encouragement and said it would get easier but we were 4.5 miles up at this point and it was a straight uphill climb the entire way.

Blue Lakes of Mt.Sneffels

This is what we came to see. It's even better in person. This is the first of three lakes on the mountain. Me and my girlfriend were both exhausted so we set up camp here and enjoyed some amazing views overnight. I planned the trip out for June so that the Milky-Way would be visible in the night sky. 10/10 would recommend.
